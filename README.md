An intelligent tourism companion designed to help visitors discover attractions, restaurants, events, and hidden gems across Saudi Arabia.

## ✨ Overview

Hawlik is a tourism planning and navigation application that provides personalized recommendations based on user interests and budget. The app helps tourists efficiently plan their trips and explore Saudi Arabia through one unified platform.

  <img src="Hawlik.png" alt="Hawlik App Overview" width="100%" />

## 🎯 Problem

Tourists often face challenges such as:

- Scattered tourism information across multiple platforms
- Difficulty finding suitable attractions
- Unplanned days during their trips
- Lack of personalized recommendations

## 💡 Solution

Hawlik combines attractions, events, restaurants, and services into one platform and provides:

- Personalized recommendations
- Interactive maps
- Trip planning
- Saved places
- Interest-based exploration
- Real-time nearby locations

## 🚀 Features

### Discover Places
Browse nearby attractions based on your interests.

### Smart Recommendations
Get personalized suggestions based on:
- Budget
- Interests
- Location

### Trip Planning
Create and organize travel plans easily.

### Saved Places
Save locations for future visits.

### Interactive Maps
Explore attractions with an intuitive map experience.

### Accessibility
Clear icons, visual cues, and easy navigation.

## 📊 Research Insights

Our user research included 85 participants and revealed that:

- 53% struggled due to insufficient information
- 26% faced difficulties organizing trips
- 21% experienced poor guidance and user experience
